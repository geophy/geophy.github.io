Welcome

I'm Xie Fan and you're currently browsing my website.

I'm a full-time research scientist at Institude of Geophysics, China Earthquake Administration (CEA-IGP) and Seismic Observation and Geophysical Imaging Lab (SOGI)

My research interest is to study nonlinear phenomenon and damage evolution on concrete and rocks using ultrasound, develop acoustic methods for purpose of material's non-destructive testing (NDT) and/or structural health monitoring (SHM).

Thanks for visiting and enjoy the site.
